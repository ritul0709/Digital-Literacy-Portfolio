Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Do not click on suspicious links received through email, SMS, or social media.
- Always verify the sender’s email address or phone number before responding.
- Use strong and unique passwords for different accounts.
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) for added security.

- Never share your OTP, PIN, or passwords with anyone, even if they claim to be from a bank.
- Always check UPI app notifications carefully before approving any payment request.
- Do not scan unknown QR codes, as they can lead to unauthorized transactions.
- Regularly check your bank statements and UPI transaction history.

- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions or sensitive activities.
- Keep your devices updated with the latest security patches and antivirus software.

Reporting Cybercrime in India:
- Report incidents at the National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
- Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 for immediate assistance

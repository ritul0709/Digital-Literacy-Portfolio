Case Study: Phishing Attack

Phishing is a common type of cybercrime where attackers try to trick people into sharing sensitive information like passwords, bank details, or OTPs.

In this case, a user received an email that looked like it was from a bank. The email asked the user to click on a link and enter their login details. The user trusted the email and entered the information.

As a result, the attacker gained access to the user’s account and caused financial loss.

Impact:
- Loss of money
- Personal data stolen
- Privacy breach

Prevention:
- Do not click on unknown links
- Never share OTP or passwords
- Check email authenticity
- Use strong passwords
- Enable two-factor authentication
